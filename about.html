MARCO OCAMPO

Marco is a photographer, author, director, producer, and on-camera talent who has worked on editorial, publishing, advertising, content creation, documentary, and feature film projects. He is also an internationally recognized workshop instructor and columnist on the subject of visuals.

Marco is also an Executive Producer for the film company Abra Dos Pictures and has recently completed work on his first full-length feature film, Team Marco.

Clients include The New York Times, Conde Nast, Meredith Corporation, Hearst Corporation, Apple, Adobe, CreativeLIVE, Disney, Hay House Publishing, Clarkson Potter, Harper Collins, Norton, and Grey Advertising among others.
